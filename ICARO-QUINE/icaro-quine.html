HOW TO USE ABC FLIX

A monochrome dot-matrix machine for making animated films on a 128×96 grid.

A
ADD: Mount images, load video (auto-ripped to frames), activate camera, or generate an SMPTE film leader.
B
BUILD: Write BEFLIX-128 script or describe an animation and tap GENERATE. RUN SCRIPT to compile frames.
C
DRAW: Ink directly on canvas even while playing! The floating toolbar stays on screen — no panel needed. Pen, box, or eraser. 8 intensity levels.
D
SAVE: Export WebM, MP4, GIF, still PNG, or contact sheet.
STATUS BAR: The top bar always shows current frame, active tool, ink color, and FPS — no need to open panels to check state.
